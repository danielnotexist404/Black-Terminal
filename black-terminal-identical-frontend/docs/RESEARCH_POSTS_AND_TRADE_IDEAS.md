# Research Posts And Trade Ideas

## Research Objects

Research composer modes expose title, executive summary, body, symbols, asset class, timeframe, directional bias, attachments, visibility, comments policy, and disclaimer only when relevant. Long research collapses in-feed and expands on demand.

## Trade Ideas

Trade ideas record entry or zone, invalidation, targets, conviction, timeframe, bias, and lifecycle. Supported lifecycle states are Active, Updated, Invalidated, Target Reached, Closed, and Archived. Historical lifecycle claims are never silently overwritten: the prior post revision and an explicit lifecycle update object are retained.

Every trade idea is labeled as research and discussion rather than guaranteed advice.

## Indicators And Strategies

Only records whose canonical publication row is public may be attached. The server reconstructs public card metadata from the publication table and ignores client-supplied implementation metadata. Indicator or strategy source code is never serialized into the social attachment.

Chart snapshots share the secure image pipeline and preserve approved symbol, timeframe, venue, indicator-list, timestamp, and annotation metadata. Users review the image before publication; account balances, credentials, and order identifiers are not captured automatically.
