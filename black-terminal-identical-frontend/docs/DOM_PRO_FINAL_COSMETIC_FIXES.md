# DOM Pro Final Cosmetic Fixes

## Camera Controls

Center, Fit, Follow and Explore now occupy a dedicated four-track control group with stable minimum widths, uniform height, complete labels and explicit hover/active states. The stats strip has a track for every control and scrolls only when the full workstation cannot fit safely.

## Heatmap Wall Labels

Permanent BUY WALL and SELL WALL labels are measured and clipped to their actual strip rectangle. Text is vertically centered, padded horizontally and shortened to BUY or SELL when the wall is too thin. Supply strips use white text; bright demand strips use dark text. Full wall details remain in the existing hover inspection path.

## Bottom Row

The factory bottom row is 30% of the available cockpit. Depth Chart, Liquidity Flow Delta and Execution have independent split ratios. The upper analytical region receives the remaining space and retains its two-row Tape/CVD and Metrics/Performance arrangement.

## Proof

- deterministic split, constraint, collapse, maximize, TIF, leverage and wall-bound tests;
- 1920x1080 and 1280x800 browser captures;
- resize, collapsed, maximized and compact execution captures;
- DOM Pro panel, venue execution, performance, visual and production build gates.

No market calculation, wall detection, worker cadence, camera state or execution semantics were changed by this cosmetic chapter.
