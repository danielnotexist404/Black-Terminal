One architectural recommendation

I would add one more module beyond what you've described: a Master Account.

Instead of your chart sending orders directly to investor accounts:

Charts
    │
Strategy / Manual Trade
    │
Master Execution Engine
    │
Risk Engine
    │
Allocation Engine
    │
Broker Adapters
    │
──────────────────────────────
│        │        │
Your     Inv A    Inv B
Bybit    Bybit    Hyperliquid
│        │        │
Exchange Exchange Exchange

This gives you one-click trading while allowing different sizing, leverage limits, and risk rules per investor account. It's also much easier to audit, replay, and extend in the future.

With the platform you've already shown, these additions would move it from being an advanced charting terminal toward a full desktop execution and portfolio management workstation. The remaining engineering work is primarily around connectivity, security, execution, and risk management rather than chart rendering.
