# Obsidian Regulatory Questions

Status: issue-spotting checklist only; not legal advice or a legal conclusion.

Smart-contract enforcement, wallet use and “self-custody” language do not automatically remove regulatory, contractual, tax or consumer-protection obligations. No public-capital launch is approved by technical documentation.

## Required counsel workstreams

- Does pooled vault participation constitute a collective investment undertaking, fund, security, transferable claim or another regulated product?
- Do strategy selection and restricted execution constitute portfolio management, fund management, copy trading, reception/transmission or execution of client orders?
- How are perpetuals/derivatives classified, and who may offer, market, manage or access them?
- Does contract or adapter control amount to custody, control, safeguarding or client-asset responsibility?
- Which entity is operator, protocol developer, arranger, marketer, fee recipient, data controller and complaints handler?
- Are performance fees permitted and what disclosure, calculation, audit and conflict rules apply?
- What are the implications of locks, delayed settlement, impairment, liquidation and emergency gating?
- Are investors consumers, retail clients, professional investors, qualified investors or limited to another category?
- What KYC/AML, sanctions, source-of-funds, travel-rule, wallet-screening and suspicious-activity duties apply?
- Which countries must be excluded; how are location, solicitation and reverse-solicitation treated?
- What disclosures, risk warnings, suitability/appropriateness, cooling-off, complaints and marketing approvals are required?
- How do MiCA, MiFID II, AIFMD and national implementations interact with the exact token, service and governance model?
- What stablecoin, issuer-freeze, derivatives and market-abuse rules apply?
- What entity, investor and transaction tax reporting applies to deposits, shares, fees, funding, trades and redemptions?
- What GDPR/data-protection roles, lawful bases, retention, wallet-address profiling and cross-border transfer rules apply?
- What licensing, capital, insurance, audit, recordkeeping and supervisory reporting obligations apply?

## Evidence package for counsel

Provide jurisdiction/entity map, user journey, authority matrix, funds flow, contracts and upgrade map, venue/adapter model, fee examples, disclosures, investor categories, marketing language, incident plan and data-flow diagram. Counsel conclusions must identify jurisdiction, facts, assumptions, effective date and launch constraints.

## Official starting references

- EU Markets in Crypto-Assets Regulation (EU) 2023/1114: https://eur-lex.europa.eu/eli/reg/2023/1114/oj
- MiFID II Directive 2014/65/EU: https://eur-lex.europa.eu/eli/dir/2014/65/oj
- AIFMD Directive 2011/61/EU: https://eur-lex.europa.eu/eli/dir/2011/61/oj
- GDPR Regulation (EU) 2016/679: https://eur-lex.europa.eu/eli/reg/2016/679/oj

These are issue-list sources, not determinations of applicability. Qualified counsel must check consolidated law, national implementation, regulator guidance and facts at review time.
