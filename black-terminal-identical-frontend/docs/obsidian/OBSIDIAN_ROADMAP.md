# Obsidian Roadmap

Status: research and assurance sequence, not a delivery commitment.

## Stage 0 — Constitution

Resolve authority, forbidden powers, model choice, accounting, lifecycle, emergency, governance and legal facts. Produce reviewed specifications and an executable reference model before smart-contract work.

## Stage 1 — External research

### Technical/market landscape

Catalogue strategy vaults, managed on-chain vaults, copy/social trading, tokenized funds, performance-fee protocols, time-locked vaults, perpetual DEX vaults, delegated smart accounts and account abstraction. For each, record chain, custody, operator powers, accounting, locks, fees, adapters, governance, audits, incidents and current status from primary evidence.

Separate:

- existing primitives and standards;
- known production/research implementations;
- potentially distinctive combinations in Project Obsidian;
- claims requiring patent or intellectual-property counsel.

No “first” or “never existed” claim is permitted without a dated competitive, prior-art and patent landscape review.

### Venue/chain research

Compare one-chain candidates and on-chain perpetual venues for contract interface, custody/margin model, oracle, liquidation, callbacks, governance/upgrades, withdrawal path, historical incidents, test environment, audits, liquidity and jurisdictional constraints. Research does not equal approval.

## Stage 2 — Executable specification

Build an offline mathematical/state reference model, property generators, accounting vectors and threat-to-invariant matrix. This is not a production contract.

## Stage 3 — Minimal test contracts

Only after authorization: implement deposit/tranche/epoch/accounting and a mock adapter, then follow the full assurance plan. No real venue or valuable assets.

## Stage 4 — Smallest viable public testnet experiment

One chain selected by research; mock USDC or official no-value test asset; one immutable/capped group vault; one term version; one 90-day-equivalent compressed test epoch; isolated tranches; one mock or verified testnet venue adapter; BTC/ETH test markets only; leverage capped at 2× or lower; invitation-only addresses; no bridges; no upgrade during experiment.

Demonstrate: two investors enter at different NAVs; operator cannot withdraw/arbitrary-call; mandate expires/revokes; gain/loss/HWM fees reconcile; maturity only enables queued redemption; pause blocks exposure increase; ambiguous intent cannot duplicate; adapter disable works; emergency claim preserves beneficiary.

Testnet success is engineering evidence, not authorization for real capital.

## Stage 5 — Audited restricted pilot

Requires legal clearance, both audits, bounty readiness, governance/incident rehearsals and hard caps. Exact asset value and participant type remain counsel decisions.

## Stage 6 — Gradual scope expansion

Add one dimension at a time—capital, market, venue, chain or wallet feature—with a new risk assessment and assurance evidence. Obsidian Account and Obsidian Wallet remain later, independent workstreams.
